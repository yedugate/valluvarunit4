Section 1

Overview

Nutrition plays a crucial role in optimising athletic performance, recovery, and overall health. Athletes require tailored diets to meet increased energy demands, support muscle repair, and maintain hydration.

Hydration

Hydration is critical for fluid balance, thermoregulation, and fatigue prevention. Hydrate before, during, and after sessions; replace electrolytes during prolonged or intense activity.

Section 7

Supplements

  • Use cautiously; prioritise whole foods first.
  • Common options: whey protein, creatine, caffeine, electrolytes.
  • Always consider safety and sport doping regulations.
