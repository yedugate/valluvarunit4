Section 4

Hydration

  • Adequate fluids are critical to maintain performance and prevent heat stress, especially in hot/humid work.
  • Use water primarily; consider ORS/electrolyte drinks during heavy sweating or long shifts.
  • Rehydrate after work; include sodium and potassium foods if large sweat losses occur.

Nutrition Tips for Hard Working Men

  • Eat balanced meals at regular intervals to maintain energy.
  • Include protein in every meal to support recovery.
  • Limit ultra‚Äëprocessed foods and sugary snacks.
  • Plan fluids during and after activity; replace electrolytes when needed.
  • Consider supplements only based on individual assessment with a healthcare professional.
