Section 2

Nutritional Importance

  • Often lower in calories, helpful for weight management when used judiciously.
  • Provide vitamins, minerals, antioxidants, and fibre without added fat (especially fruits/vegetables).
Some fat is essential for:
  • Absorption of vitamins A, D, E, K
  • Hormone production
  • Cell membrane integrity
  • Satiety and flavour
